Dataset Title : Multivariate Water Quality Assessment Dataset for Water Quality Index (WQI) Prediction

Usage of Dataset
This dataset is used for analyzing, modeling, and predicting the Water Quality Index (WQI) using multiple physicochemical and biological water parameters. It is suitable for machine learning and deep learning–based water quality monitoring, environmental assessment, and decision-support systems.

Dataset Information



Dataset Name:Water Quality Parameters and WQI Dataset



Source:Derived from monitored water quality observations (sensor-based and laboratory-measured environmental data)



Domain:Environmental Engineering / Water Resources / Data Science



Task:Water Quality Index (WQI) prediction



Problem Type:Supervised Learning – Regression



File Format:CSV (Comma-Separated Values)



Dataset Link:https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IRq3wJwWpM0Mtm6c5qcsRuOEIMDf3kKJ/view?usp=drive_link



**Dataset Overview**



Total Records:295 samples



Labeled Records:295 (fully labeled)



Classes:Not applicable (continuous WQI values)



Annotation Type:Numerical annotation (computed Water Quality Index values)



Why This Dataset?
Includes both physicochemical and biological parameters, offering a realistic representation of water quality.

Suitable for regression modeling and performance comparison of ML/DL algorithms.

Helps in early detection of water pollution and supports real-time water quality forecasting.

Ideal for academic projects, research papers, and smart environmental monitoring systems.


Features Used



Feature 1

Temperature – Indicates thermal condition of water affecting chemical reactions.

Feature 2

Dissolved Oxygen (DO) – Essential for aquatic life and a key pollution indicator.

Feature 3

pH – Measures acidity/alkalinity of water.

Summary

This dataset consists of 295 fully labeled water samples with 9 input features and 1 output variable (WQI). It is well-suited for supervised regression tasks, environmental analytics, and AI-based water quality prediction systems. The dataset enables accurate assessment of pollution levels and supports the development of intelligent water monitoring solutions.
